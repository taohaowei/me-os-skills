## English

### What this is / is not

This project is a **personal memory and conversation-organizing framework** — it helps you record, organize, and revisit personal topics and relationships in a structured way, and provides a protocol for an AI assistant to "load different perspectives per topic."

This project is **NOT**:

- ❌ a medical device, nor any regulated medical or health product;
- ❌ a diagnostic tool — it does not and cannot provide any medical, psychiatric, or psychological diagnosis;
- ❌ a treatment plan — it does not provide medical, medication, therapeutic, or clinical advice;
- ❌ a substitute for a licensed physician, certified counselor, clinical psychologist, or psychiatrist.

### About the "Health Recorder" subsystem

The "Health Recorder" subsystem **only organizes and archives information** — it records the symptoms, visits, medications, and other entries that you voluntarily provide, so you can review them yourself or bring them to a clinician.

- It **only records; it does not diagnose, interpret, or prescribe**;
- Nothing it organizes **constitutes medical advice**;
- For any health decision, rely on **in-person care from a licensed physician**.

### About psychological and emotional topics

The "counseling lens" in this framework is a **perspective for organizing conversation and self-reflection**. It is not psychotherapy, and it does not replace professional mental-health services of any kind.

> 🆘 **If you are experiencing a mental-health crisis or having thoughts of harming yourself or others, stop using this tool immediately and contact your local professional services or emergency number.**
> See `config/crisis-hotlines.{{locale}}.md` for contact information.
